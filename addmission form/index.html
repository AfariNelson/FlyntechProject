Addmission Process

To be considered for Addmission to St.Elizabeth Pre-School prospective pupils must be;
      At least two(2) years old
      Potty trained
      for kingderarten 1, must be 4 years old on or before September
      for kingderarten 2, must be 5 years old on or before September
      Pupil will be tested to ascertain his or her academic standard
Primary 1
      Pupil must be 5 or 6 years old with a solid kingderarten foundation
      Pupil will be tested to ascertain his or her academic standard
Primary 2 to 6 (on transfer from other schools
      Pupil will be tested to ascertain his or her academic standard
      Cumulative record book of Pupil should be submitted to St.Elizabeth on addmission
Junior High School
Junior High School requirement is same as Primary 2 to 6
