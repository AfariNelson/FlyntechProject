ADMISSION LEVELS

We admit students from the preschool level through to the JHS level.

PRESCHOOL

For your ward to be admitted to the preschool level he/she must be between the age range of 6months to 5 year old.

PRIMARY

For your ward to be admitted to the primary level he/she must be at least 5years old.

JHS

For your ward to qualify to be admitted in JHS level he/she must be at least 10years old or pass the necessary admission exams.
